22 Hours With Simon Puffett Sensei
16 Oct 2020 at 17:00 – 17 Oct 2020 at 15:00

Our first ever and free ADULTS sleep-over! Starting with a children’s class (adults are welcome to join in) from 5-6pm followed by an adult’s class from 6-8pm, pizza, talking, sleeping on the mat, more training on Saturday and time together.

Expect lots of good training, opportunities to ask questions and discuss details you always wondered about, time to relax together and stuff for lasting memories. This workshop is predominantly geared towards instructors understanding and teaching the basics to students. However, all grades including beginners are welcome to join in and learn.
