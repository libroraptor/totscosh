Welcome!

Visit our dōjō in Tahunanui, Nelson and experience aikidō.

Feel free to come and watch a class and ask our friendly instructors about joining in.

The first class you train in is free.

Aikido is for everyone, no matter your age or size. Aikidō is a non-competitive Japanese martial art. It is dynamic and powerful, but its techniques do not rely on strength.

Learn self-defence and be part of a small, friendly club. Gain confidence and widen your spiritual horizons, or work on your fitness, flexibility and overall health. Join in with partner exercises, wooden weapon work, and meditation practice.

We look forward to seeing you at Aikidō Nelson.
